Welcome to GESAN

Geomatics Engineering Students' Association of Nepal is the guild established in 2069 B.S. by students of Geomatics Engineering at Institute of Engineering, Pashchimanchal Campus, Pokhara.

Since its establishment, GESAN has been a podium for the students to get engaged in activities like trainings, seminars, workshops, expertise meets and flourish their skills.
